The Property

The whole property is yours

One booking.
Everything included.

Oakhaven is a fully private rental — there are no shared spaces and no other guests. When you book, you have the log cabin, the cottage, the veranda, the braai area and the garden entirely to your group.

The property sleeps up to 12 guests (10 adults + 2 children 12 & under) across four bedrooms and three bathrooms, with a shared kitchen, living and dining area in the main log cabin.

Perfect for families, groups of friends, or anyone who wants the whole place to themselves in one of the most beautiful corners of South Africa.

Sleeping arrangements

Four bedrooms across the property.

Every room has its own character — from the warmth of the tartan-clad main bedroom to the loft's four singles and the cottage's private ensuite.

Main Bedroom
Queen bed + bunk beds  ·  Sleeps 2 adults + 2 children (12 & under)

The heart of the log cabin — warm timber walls, tartan bedding and morning light through the windows. The bunk beds make it perfect for families travelling with younger children.

Queen bed Kids bunk Log cabin
Second Bedroom
Queen bed  ·  Sleeps 2 adults

A beautifully appointed room in the main log cabin, with warm lamp light, a reading corner and views of the garden. Ideal for a couple or two guests sharing.

Queen bed Log cabin
The Loft
4 single beds  ·  Sleeps 4 adults or children

Running the full length of the cabin upstairs, the loft is a generous space with four single beds, large wooden windows and the dramatic beamed ceiling of the log cabin above. Great for kids or a group of friends.

4 singles Loft level Log cabin
The Cottage
Queen bed + ensuite  ·  Sleeps 2 adults

A separate cottage with its own character — raw corrugated iron, exposed brick, a sliding barn door and an ensuite bathroom. Private and quiet, with its own fireplace and morning light through the triangular window. Perfect for a couple wanting a little space from the rest of the group.

Living spaces

More than just bedrooms.

The Lounge

Rattan chairs, a wood-burning log burner, wooden walls and the kind of armchair you disappear into. The heart of the log cabin on a cold evening.

The Veranda

A long outdoor dining table, Edison bulb lighting and mountain views as far as you can see. The best seat in the house — especially at sunset.

The Staircase & Loft Hall

Built from actual logs, the staircase leads up to the loft level — dramatic, rustic and full of character. The loft hallway above has the beamed ceiling at its most impressive.
