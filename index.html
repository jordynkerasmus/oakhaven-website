Oakhaven is a fully private rental — the log cabin, the cottage, the deck and the garden. Everything is yours for the duration of your stay. No shared spaces, no other guests. Just your group and the mountains.

Whether you've come to fish the rivers, ride into the foothills, walk until your legs give in, or simply sit on the deck with a cup of something warm — the Southern Drakensberg rewards every pace.

Adventure on your doorstep

There's always something to do.

From fly fishing at dawn to stargazing after dinner — the Southern Drakensberg doesn't run out of things to offer.

Fly Fishing
2 min from Oakhaven
160 km of rivers and 60 dams. One of South Africa's two premier trout destinations. Day permits from R200/rod.
Hiking
3 km from Oakhaven
Over 70 km of marked trails on the Sani Spoors network — family loops to full-day Drakensberg traverses.
Horse Riding
7 km from Oakhaven
Khotso Horse Trails — 1,300 hectares of foothill country. Summit Eland's Peak at 1,800 m and watch eagles soar below.
Golf
1.5 km from Oakhaven
9-hole, par-72 course at Underberg Country Club. Visitors welcome — weekly competitions on Wednesdays and Saturdays.
River Tubing
7 km from Oakhaven
5 km of the Mzimkulu River through Khotso farm. Exhilarating summer fun for the whole group.
Sani Pass
33 km from Oakhaven
The legendary 4×4 pass to Lesotho — rising to 2,874 m and the highest pub in Africa.
